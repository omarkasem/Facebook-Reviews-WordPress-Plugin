== Description ==
Dispaly your Facebook pages Reviews in a beautiful way with a lot of options to customize the reviews as you like, in a slider facebook reviews or a regular facebook reviews, create unlimited shortcodes, widgets and wp rest api urls, You can display your facebook reviews in your already designed template, Choose Main color of the reviews, Hide any reviews less than 5 or 4 stars etc, Hide blank reviews that doesn't have any text in them, Display your reviews in 1,2,3,4 responisve bootstrap columns.

== Awesome Features ==
*   Create unlimted facebook reviews with shortcodes
*   Create unlimted facebook reviews with WP REST API URLS
*   Already Desgined Template for displaying your facebook reviews
*   You can display your facebook reviews in your already designed template
*	You can display your facebook reviews with widgets
*	You can display your feeds in a slider or regular mode.
*	Choose Main color of the reviews
*	Hide any reviews less than 5 or 4 stars etc
*	Hide blank reviews that doesn't have any text in them
*	Display your reviews in 1,2,3,4 responisve bootstrap columns.
*	Schema.org supported & SEO Optimized.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `ultimate-facebook-reviews.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('plugin_name_hook'); ?>` in your templates
